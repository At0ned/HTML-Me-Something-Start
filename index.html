Welcome to Gulf Shores Alabama!


"Where a 5 minute drive can take you from the middle of Hicksville to the beach" - me

Back in August, 2021, my family took a trip down to Gulf Shores, Alabama. It was my first time ever going to the Gulf of Mexico. The trip had a little bit of everything.

Pirates Island


Mini golf, a family favorite. I feel like every mini golf course has a pirate theme.

Fishing Charter


We went on a fishing charter for Spanish and King Mackerel. We went about a mile offshore and anchored down. Within an hour we caught over 20 mackerel. Both of my brothers hooked the same 9 foot tiger shark at different times. It wasn't a huge shark, but it managed to pull the boat effortlessly while swimming away. Both of their lines snapped after about 5 minutes. One of the many reasons I'll never mess with the Ocean.

After this trip, I realized I wanted to live on the coast eventually. I can't get enough of Gulfs/oceans. I love the fishing, culture, and the sound of waves hitting the beach. A beautiful wonder of God's creation.
